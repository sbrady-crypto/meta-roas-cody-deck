ActionWhat we're doing about it

Four actions.

01

Exclude Audience Network — account-wide.

Reclaim ~$40K spend. Removes 46M garbage impressions from training data. Cleanest single fix in the report.

02

Switch top ASC campaigns to 7d-click + 1d-view.

Restores algorithm signal lost to the attribution change. Start with IA Partnership, IA CostCap, Partnership Winners.

03

Rebalance VO/CO mix.

Pull spend back into VO incrementally. Target ~25% VO share (where it was pre-shift).

04

Report blended + IA ROAS alongside click.

Click ROAS in isolation overstates the decline by 10–15 points. Update weekly pacing template.

Bottom line

The drop is real. But it's mostly measurement and mix — not demand.

Of the 26 reported ROAS points lost, ~16–20 are measurement reshuffle and structural overcorrection we can recover. Real residual decline is ~6–10 points — solvable with the four actions.
